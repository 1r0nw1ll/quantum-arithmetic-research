### NTRU Lattice Vulnerability ([515])

Full proof + empirical verification in one cert. Mechanism: when 3|m, `qa_step` reduces mod m, so mod-3 residues follow the direct mod-3 recursion, which has period ≤8. Any NTRU key polynomial of degree N>8 built from such orbits is periodic. Empirical: real NTRU (N=83, q=256, fpylll 0.6.4, real LLL/BKZ), m=9 (3|9): 6/10 LLL-broken. Random keys: 0/10 LLL-broken. m=80 (gcd=1): 0–1/12 broken (matches random).

Corrects an earlier informal claim that QA harmonic filtering mitigated lattice attacks — that material never specified an actual lattice basis.

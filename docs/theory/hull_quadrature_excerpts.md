## Introduction

### #introduction

> "Pi has the correct and exact value of 20,612 parts of circumference to 6,561 parts of diameter."

— Hull's central claim, attributed forward from Parker's 19th-century work. This is asserted in the introduction as the "solution to the Quadrature of the Circle".

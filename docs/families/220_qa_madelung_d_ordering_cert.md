## Claim

Atomic subshells `(n, l)` with `n ≥ 1` and `0 ≤ l ≤ n − 1`, identified as QA coordinates `(b, e) = (n, l)`, satisfy:

1. **QA identification**: `d = b + e = n + l` is the Madelung quantum number.
2. **Aufbau = QA (d, −e) sort**: Madelung's filling rule is exactly ordering by `(d, −e)` ascending on the `(b, e)` lattice.
3. **Exact reproduction through 36 subshells**: the Janet-extended Madelung sequence (1s, 2s, 2p, 3s, 3p, 4s, 3d, 4p, 5s, 4d, 5p, 6s, 4f, 5d, 6p, 7s, 5f, 6d, 7p, 8s, 5g, 6f, 7d, 8p, 9s, 6g, 7f, 8d, 9p, 10s, 6h, 7g, 8f, 9d, 10p, 11s) is generated by the QA sort with zero mismatches.
4. **Selection rule**: the deterministic (b,e)-lattice walk — within-d antidiagonal step `(b, e) → (b+1, e−1)` and between-d jump `(b, 0) → (⌈(b+2)/2⌉, ⌊b/2⌋)` — holds for all 35 consecutive transitions in the 36-subshell sequence.
5. **Derived structure**: subshell population = `4e + 2`; period `k` population = `2⌈(k+1)/2⌉²` (giving `{2, 8, 8, 18, 18, 32, 32, 50, …}`, matching the physical periodic table periods 1–7 plus Janet predictions for 8+); shell-`n` total = `2n²`.

## QA diagonal class

**d-ordering walk across all d-classes.** This is a distinct class from:

- [217] Fuller VE — `b=e` diagonal D_1 (self-similar ray)
- [218] Haramein — fixed-d hyperbola (Q(√5))
- [219] Fibonacci Resonance — T-orbit Fibonacci shift

The Madelung trajectory visits **every** d-class in ascending order rather than sitting on one distinguished diagonal. The structural claim is about the *ordering principle itself*, not residence on a diagonal.

Crucially: **NOT a ℤ[φ] / Q(√5) structure.** No Fibonacci, no φ, no quadratic form on deltas. Polynomial/combinatorial, per the morning OB note "atomic 2n² is NOT D_k (polynomial vs exponential)".

## What this cert does NOT claim

- No derivation of the Madelung rule from the Schrödinger equation. Madelung is empirical; QA describes its combinatorial shape, not its physical cause.
- No prediction of the Madelung anomalies (Cr, Cu, Pd, Ag, lanthanides/actinides). Those live in the permissibility/semantic layer.
- No claim that `a = n + 2l` has an established physical interpretation. Open mapping question — see theory note §5.3.
- No ℤ[φ] / Q(√5) structure. Different QA class than [217]/[218]/[219].

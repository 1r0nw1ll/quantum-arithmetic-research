# Field Structure Theory (FST) Documentation

This directory contains research documents exploring **Field Structure Theory** as proposed by Don Briddell, and its integration with **Quantum Arithmetic (QA)** systems.

## Overview

Field Structure Theory (FST) proposes that the universe is built from chiral loops of action, where the interaction of these loops in three dimensions gives rise to fields that can condense into matter and deploy into energy. This repository organizes FST-related research, mathematical frameworks, and computational simulations.

## Key Concepts

### FST Fundamentals
- **Chiral Loops of Action**: Fundamental building blocks of the universe
- **Tometry**: New geometric analytic for mapping loop structures and field interactions
- **Fractal Hierarchy**: Recursive scaling from quantum particles to cosmic macro-structures
- **Non-Intersecting Fields**: Closed loops that interact without crossing, maintaining field integrity

### QA-FST Integration
- **Loop as Mass**: QA elliptic tuples represent modular loops (mod-24, mod-72, mod-144)
- **Twist as Energy**: Torsional shifts in QA tuple phase space encode energy states
- **Harmonic Resonance**: Modular closure patterns match particle mass hierarchies
- **E8 Embeddings**: 248-dimensional exceptional Lie algebra as geometric unity framework

### Mathematical Models
- **Toroidal Resonance**: Harmonic field embeddings in toroidal geometry
- **Modular Arithmetic**: Prime residue cycles revealing natural periodicity
- **Scalar Potentials**: Whittaker decomposition of EM fields into bidirectional scalars
- **Meson Mass Law**: E = (300λ + 10)/100 where λ is twist coefficient

### Related Theories
- **Bearden Scalar EM**: Active vacuum engineering, Lorentz gauge breaking, energy extraction
- **Whittaker Potentials**: 1903 decomposition of EM field into scalar potential functions F and G
- **Buckminster Fuller**: Synergetic geometry influence on Briddell's early FST work (1966)

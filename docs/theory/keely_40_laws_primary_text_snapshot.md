# Keely — *Forty Laws of Sympathetic Vibratory Physics* (c.1893) — Primary Quotes

**Author:** John Ernst Worrell Keely (1827–1898)
**Publication (indirect):** Clara Bloomfield-Moore, *Keely and His Discoveries* (1893); transcribed on svpwiki.com/Laws-of-Being-Propositions by Dale Pond

## Law 1 — Law of Matter and Force

### #law-1

> "There exists an infinite and unchangeable quantity of atomoles, the base of all matter; these are in a state of constant vibratory motion."

## Law 6 — Law of Sympathetic Oscillation

### #law-6

> "Coherent aggregates immersed in a medium pulsating at their natural pitch simultaneously oscillate with the same frequency, whether the pitch of the medium be a unison, or any harmonic of the fundamental pitch."

## Law 11 — Law of Force

### #law-11

> "Energy manifests itself in three forms: Creative (vibrating aggregate), Transmissive (propagation of isochronous waves), Attractive (action upon other aggregates capable of vibrating in unisons or harmony)."

## Law 17 — Law of Transformation of Forces

### #law-17

> "All forces are different forms of Universal Energy unlike in their period-frequency, merging into each other by imperceptible increments; each form representing the compass of 21 octaves."

## Law 18 — Law of Atomic Pitch

### #law-18

> "Atoms have each a different and definite pitch, at which they naturally vibrate."

## Proposition III

### #prop-iii

> "The circle is the natural basis or beginning of all area, and the square being made so in mathematical science, is artificial and arbitrary."

— Parker's foundational claim that the circle (modular cycle) is prior to the square (Cartesian quadrance). Anchors the QA-discrete-primary / continuous-projection position.

## Proposition VII

### #prop-vii

> "Because the circle is the primary shape in nature... circumference and radius, and not the square of diameter, are the only natural and legitimate elements of area."

— Parker's argument that circumference × radius, not diameter², is the natural area basis. In QA terms this selects C (circumference-class) and F (radius-class) as the primitive area elements, giving L = CF/12 on the 16-identities cert.

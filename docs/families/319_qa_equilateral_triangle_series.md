## The equilateral triangle from a BEDA tuple

Every BEDA 4-tuple (b,e,d,a) generates an equilateral triangle with all sides equal to W:

| Measurement | Formula | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| W | de + da = d(e+a) | side length (all three sides) |
| F | ab | shorter base segment |
| Y | ed + ea = e(d+a) | longer base segment |
| Z | e² + da | dividing-line length (vertex to opposite base) |

The base divides into F and Y with F+Y=W (certified by [152]).

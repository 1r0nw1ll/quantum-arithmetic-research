## Chapter 1 — Doubling

### #ch1-doubling

> "If the first integer is 2-par, the fourth integer will be 4-par. If the first integer is 4-par, then the fourth will be 2-par."

— QA-2, Chapter 1 "Doubling", on the parity structure of female-root Quantum Numbers.

### #ch1-doubling-p7

> "The Quantum Number for the orbit of Halley's Comet is 1, 29, 30, 59. Its ratio between the perigee and apogee is 1:59. This is a much elongated elliptical orbit."

— QA-2, Chapter 1 "Doubling", page 7, worked Quantum Number example.

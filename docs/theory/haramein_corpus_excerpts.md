## Haramein 2013 — *Quantum Gravity and the Holographic Mass*

**Journal:** Physical Science International Journal 3(4):270-292
**Authors:** Nassim Haramein
**On-disk:** `Documents/haramein_rsf/quantum_gravity_holographic_mass_2013.pdf` (345KB, ~22pp; acquired via OSF 5ed8c zip → QGHM.pdf)

### #psu-definition (p.272, Introduction last paragraph)

> "we generalize the holographic principle by utilizing a spherical Planck unit rather than a surface area Planck unit, as a minimum-size vacuum energy oscillator on which information encodes, which we term 'Planck spherical unit' (PSU)"

### #psu-volume-eq5 (p.274, eq. 5)

> "a spherical PSU of radius l_r has a volume of V_ls = (4/3) π l_r^3 ... or V_ls = 2.210462 × 10^-99 cm^3"

### #holographic-mass-eq9 (p.274-275, eq. 9-13)

> "m_h = (R/η) m_l ... We find that a simple relationship of the internal PSUs within a given volume, to the discrete 'pixelation' of the holographic membrane surface horizon of the black hole yields what we term the holographic gravitational mass of the object which is equivalent to its classical Schwarzschild mass."

### #proton-mass-eq24 (p.278-279, eq. 24-25)

> "m_p' = η/R · m_l = 1.6714213 × 10^-24 gm ... the proton rest mass is extrapolated from the mass of PSUs on the surface divided by the number of PSUs in the interior"

### #proton-radius-eq30 (p.280, eq. 30)

> "r_p' = 4 l · m_l / m_p = 0.841236 × 10^-13 cm ... a difference of 0.000604 × 10^-13 cm from the muonic measurement of the proton charge radius"

### #phi-scale-constant-eq44 (p.283, eq. 44-45)

> "φ = ρ/R_ρ = η/R = 3.839682 × 10^-20 ... φ acts as a fundamental constant relating the background Planck vacuum fluctuation field to the cosmological and quantum scale"

### #coupling-eq52 (p.284, eq. 52)

> "F_g / F_s = G m_p m_p' / c ħ = 4 φ^2 = 5.897264 × 10^-39 ... Where 4φ² is the exact value for the coupling constant between gravitation and confinement at the proton scale or the strong interaction."

## Haramein, Brown, Val Baker 2016 — *The Unified Spacememory Network: from Cosmogenesis to Consciousness*

**Journal:** NeuroQuantology / Cosmos and History
**Authors:** Nassim Haramein, William David Brown, Amira Val Baker
**On-disk:** `Documents/haramein_rsf/unified_spacememory_network_2016.pdf` (1.4MB, 15pp; acquired via OSF dj7a4 zip → USMN.pdf)

### #spacememory-definition (p.1, Abstract)

> "the recursive information encoding feedback processes of the quantum spacetime micro-wormhole network, which we refer to as spacememory, enables memory and learning in physical systems across all scales"

### #psu-density-eq2 (p.6, eq. 1-3)

> "ρ = m_l / V_ls = 9.86 × 10^93 g/cm^3 ... The mass-energy density of any spherical body can therefore be considered in terms of its PSU packing, and can be defined as a fundamental ratio, R, of any spherical volume, V, to PSU, R = V/V_ls"

### #hubble-in-proton-eq5 (p.6, eq. 5-6)

> "R_ρ = R m_l = 2.46 × 10^55 g ... which is equivalent to the mass of the Hubble volume (observable universe), N_edd m_p = 2.62602 × 10^55 g ... indicating that the mass-energy of all the protons in the Hubble volume may be stored in the virtual mass-energy density of any one proton."

### #wormhole-count-eta (p.7)

> "we can conclude that there are ~ 4.33 × 10^40 (η) wormhole terminations over the area of the surface horizon of the proton"

### #spacetime-equals-spacememory (p.11)

> "the function of information on the structure of space during the universal evolution (spacetime = spacememory, i.e. no memory = no time)"

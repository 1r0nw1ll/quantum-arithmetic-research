# Builder Gallery

QA system mappings produced by Tier 4 members. Each entry is a validator-verified cert
covering a real system from the member's own domain.

---

## How to submit

1. Complete Exercise 04 (`EXERCISES/EXERCISE_04_YOUR_DOMAIN.md`)
2. Get a validator PASS — the output should show `[PASS]` and `checks: 0/0`
3. Copy your cert JSON and post it in the **Gallery Submissions** thread on Patreon
   (pinned in the Tier 4 feed)
4. That's it. Will verifies and adds your entry to this gallery.

Verification is the validator output — a PASS cert is the only requirement.
Human review is just adding your name to the list.

---

## How to use this gallery

Find a system in your domain. Read the cert. Adapt the encoding and generator names to your
system. Verify with the translation template.

Certs here are references, not templates. Your system will have different state labels,
different generator names, and possibly different (b,e) encodings — that is expected and correct.
